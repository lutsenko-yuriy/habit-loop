- **Finding:** `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md`'s `PactTimelineConfig`/`PactTimelineService`/`PactTimelinePage` descriptions documented a paginated design (`loadPage(pactId, pageNumber)`, a `hasMore` flag, `first_page_size`/`nth_page_size` config) that does not exist in the shipped code. - **Debt quadrant:** prudent-inadvertent — the doc simply wasn't updated when the mid-ticket redesign happened.
- **Disposition:** `docs/ARCHITECTURE.md` fixed inline (commit pending — this run's commit). The dangling test stub itself is a product decision (implement "Load more" for real, or delete the stub and close the loop) — deferred, see ledger, deadline `2026-08-15`.
