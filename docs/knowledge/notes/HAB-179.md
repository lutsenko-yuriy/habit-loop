# HAB-179: CI — two integration scenarios fail on the Android-emulator run-scenarios job

## Notes

## Debrief summary

### 2026-07-19

**What went well**
- Splitting scenarios into their own manually-dispatched CI pipeline (HAB-183) was a good call — it made it possible to isolate and iterate on this exact failure.
- Time invested in scenario timing optimizations (longer waitFor timeout, explicit pump after enterText) was worthwhile groundwork even though it didn't fully resolve the flakiness.

**What was hard or surprising**
- The fix shipped as "resolved" but the underlying flakiness is still present on CI — both scenarios pass cleanly and consistently on a local Android emulator, with no clear lead on why CI hardware behaves differently. Root cause remains unknown; tracked in GitHub issue [#297](https://github.com/lutsenko-yuriy/habit-loop/issues/297) and flagged in `README.md`'s new "Known issues" section, to revisit later.
- The ticket's debrief was skipped entirely before shipping — a one-off slip, not a systemic pattern (the equivalent multi-WU sequencing has been working).

**What to change**
- Added a debrief-check precondition to `skills/manage/ship/SKILL.md` step 1: `ship` now stops and asks before shipping a single-WU (or final-WU) ticket that has no debrief entry yet, instead of relying on the agent remembering to run `/debrief` before `/ship`.
